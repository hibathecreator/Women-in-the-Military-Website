About Me


Hiba Altaf is the author of this page. She is currently(as of 2020) a 10th grader at Brooklyn Technical High School in Brooklyn, NY. Altaf made this website as a test for her HTML/CSS/JS coding skills and as an oppurtunity for a research project. She also wanted to acknowledge the contributions of women in this men-dominated field so that their sacrificies and fight for gender-equality is not forgotten.
